The Hobbit

Book Review, Feb 27, 2024

This novel by J.R.R. Tolkien is about a trechearous adventure in a fantastic and ancient world. Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit who lives in a hole, gets recruited by Gandalf the Wizard and Thorin son of Thrain, grandson of Thror, to go reclaim the treasure of the dwarves. The treasure lies in the Lonely Mountain, guarded by Smaug the Dragon.I truly enjoyed this book and I would recommend it to anyone with a big imagination. I am so excited to begin the Lord of the Rings series!

The Da Vinci Code

Movie Review, Feb 29, 2024

This might be my favorite movie of all time. Based on Dan Brown's famous book, The Da Vinci Code is a ficticious story based on two secret socities: The Priory of Sion and The Council of Shadows. It's incredibly interesting because it provides an unproved but possible theory for the origins and location of the Holy Grail. I'd recommend it for anyone who likes a good movie to watch.
